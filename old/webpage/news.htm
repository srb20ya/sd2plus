News

 

02-23-00

Gwarsh! An update!

Anyways, once SRB2 is done, expect SD2 to incorporate a lot of the new things I've learned, plus sprites I've drawn! SD2 will finally be able to do things I couldn't have it do before.

12-08-98: Everything's been going just fine, although we've decided to limit the game to just 32 levels, instead of 100. Almost all the levels are done, and source code programming has started!

09-26-98: I've been back for a while, but forgot to update this page!!! Progress on the upcoming 32-level LITE edition of Sonic Doom 2, which only includes 32-levels, is coming along smoothly. We just have about 5 more levels to do, which most are being worked on. We've decided to not make SD2 independent from Doom 2, although the Full 100-level CD version may be independent, it will check to see if you own Doom 2.

 

We need more level-creator people!! If you are good at Doom level editing, please don't hesitate to contact us! Knowledge of TeamTNT's Boom is a plus.

05-28-98:

I'd like to congratulate SatanKillr@aol.com ! He is the ONLY one that submitted a complete entry to me, but his level is fantasic! See it as MAP17 in the 3.4 release of Sonic Doom 2!

 

Also, I've added Hydrocity Act 2 and am using an even better version of BOOM! Now baddies can't see u when u are underwater!

05-20-98:

Due to some jerk who kept bugging me about when 3.2 would be out and asked for a specific date, I am releasing it on the date I gave him, even though it is not much different from 3.1 because we are currently working on a lot of BIG stuff....can't say what it is right now. I did this to prove that you can't get more out of a version release if you rush us!!

Come on people! I have yet to receive one complete entry for the contest!!

05-05-98:

SD2BOM Version 3.1 is out, with two new levels! I included two sounds in 3.1 that hint an enemy that is about to be changed to someone cooler... try Map20 to find the sound....If you find it, e-mail me and tell me what he says and I will send u a preview picture!

The contest still goes on......send me your levels!

04-30-98:

I am holding a contest! Send me your level based on Map17 or above on the list below (04-04-98), and if I like it I will include it in Sonic Doom 2! The deadline is May 31st, so hurry!
